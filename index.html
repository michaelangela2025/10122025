Our Story - Michael's POV hi

Our story began when Angela made a grand unannounced entrance at my house to help bake a cake for a friend. She quickly took charge of the kitchen, sparking an unexpected connection. We soon connected over our shared love for video games, anime, and art. As we spent more time together, our friendship blossomed into something truly special. Now, we enjoy activities ranging from walking through parks, traveling and cooking to spamming GIFs, playing over-the-door basketball, and attempting to draw Pokémon from memory—with lots of laughs and trolling along the way.
